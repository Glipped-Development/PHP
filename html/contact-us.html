About US

Our Short Story

   

Glipped is the brainchild of Gaurav and Saumya who truly believe that we are all here for a reason, and all of us have the power to make a difference to the society we live in. Glipped is a social platform which aims to bring donors closer to the philanthropic needs of NGOs / organizations that work tirelessly to improve the social conditions of our country.

Our platform is open to individuals who are passionate about volunteering for social causes, NGOs who would like to reach out to individuals or corporates for monetary and non-monetary support and lastly, for corporates who are looking to strengthen their CSR activities.

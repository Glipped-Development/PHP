About US

Who we are? Our Short Story

   

Glipped works on ‘Connecting and Impacting’ our world, one cause at a time. The need of the hour today is to make a difference. Glipped aims at being the catalyst to social change, to be the people, for the people. Our belief is that every individual can change the destinies of a thousand, if only an effort is made.

At Glipped, we wish to be the contributor to influence the lives of many. After all, it’s only when you light a lantern for others does your own path get lit. Glipped endeavors to stir the society and bring in a wave of change. So join our movement, it’s time to use our resources for the betterment of society and the world at large!
